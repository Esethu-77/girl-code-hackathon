Welcome to Gender Bias Checker

Are you about to build an AI model? This will no doubt revolutionise your solution! However, AI models can perpetuate gender biases by learning from biased training data, replicating and reinforcing societal prejudices in their decision-making. So how do you avoid that? With this Gender Bias Assessment, you can measure whether your AI model is likely to contain gender bias. Simply fill out the form to see your score (out of 3), and receive concrete recommendations for how to improve it.
